# English Demo Script

## 30-second opener

"I built this as a portfolio-safe simulation of how I would run a Microsoft 365 Copilot adoption program. Instead of focusing on a generic AI demo, I focused on the actual responsibilities in this role: readiness assessment, use case prioritization, training and champions, adoption analytics, support, value realization, and executive facilitation."

## 7-minute flow

### 1. Start on the home screen

"The top section shows the project scope, the readiness score, and the main delivery metrics. I wanted the reviewer to understand the operating model immediately."

### 2. Show role match

"This section maps the project directly to the job description. I did that because I wanted the evidence trail to be visible rather than implied."

### 3. Show one persona-based use case

"For example, the finance close sprint is not only a prompt scenario. It has a business owner, a wave plan, success goals, and guardrails."

### 4. Show readiness and business case

"Below that, I show an ADKAR-style readiness scorecard and business-case metrics. This is how I would keep the rollout grounded in both change management and measurable value."

### 5. Show support and feedback

"I also modeled support and feedback as first-class parts of the adoption system. Champions, helpdesk, feedback themes, and Power BI views all feed the operating rhythm."

### 6. Run the planner

"If I enter a sponsor scenario, the planner produces business case actions, readiness actions, training, communications, support, feedback, facilitation, and value steps. It also converts objections into experiments with owners and dates."

### 7. Show the rollout packet preview

"Finally, the packet checker validates whether the executive review pack includes the owner, scope, business case, KPI, training, communications, support, champion model, decision log, geo scope, and language plan."

## 15-second close

"What I wanted to demonstrate is that I can connect business outcomes, change management, cross-functional execution, and practical technical delivery in one coherent Copilot adoption story."
