# 30-60-90 Day Plan

## First 30 days

- listen to current sponsors, business leads, IT owners, and adoption teams
- review existing Copilot pilots, support data, and KPI definitions
- identify the highest-potential workflows by value, readiness, and sponsor energy
- confirm where the current support and communications model is weak

## Days 31-60

- shape one or two high-confidence pilot waves
- align on business case, baseline, success KPI, and support model
- build the training path, champion rhythm, and internal communications pack
- define the steering cadence, decision log, and blocker-removal process

## Days 61-90

- run the pilots with weekly adoption, support, and quality readouts
- convert objections into experiments with owners and dates
- publish the first sponsor-facing value review
- recommend whether to scale, refine, or stop based on evidence

## Guiding principle

I would try to earn trust early by bringing structure, clarity, and momentum. My first goal would not be to launch as much as possible. My first goal would be to make sure the right launch has the right operating model behind it.
