# Copilot Business Case Playbook

## Purpose

Translate Copilot interest into a sponsor-ready business case with a baseline, a target outcome, and a decision cadence.

## Business case structure

- business problem
- target persona
- baseline workflow time or quality indicator
- target KPI after 30 days
- support model and change investment
- scale decision criteria

## Example value statements

- finance: reduce close-week recap effort while maintaining manager-approved quality
- legal: shorten first-draft recap preparation so attorneys spend more time on review and judgment
- customer service: reduce response drafting time while preserving supervisor-approved tone
- executive staff: speed up follow-through by turning recap work into visible owner-date actions

## Rules

- do not use license activation as the business case
- define value in business language
- show both adoption and outcome metrics
- review value at 30 days and 90 days

## Interview line

"My business case starts with a workflow problem and a baseline, not with a feature list. That keeps the sponsor conversation grounded in value."
